About Us

JobPortalX is an online platform that connects job seekers with employers. It offers a user-friendly interface where job seekers can search for employment opportunities based on various criteria such as job title, location, industry, and company. Employers can post job listings and search for potential candidates to fill their vacancies. JobPortalX may also provide additional features such as resume building tools, career advice articles, and interview preparation resources to assist both job seekers and employers in the recruitment process.
